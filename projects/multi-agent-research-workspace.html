Multi-Agent Research Workspace

AI-ready research workspace for collecting sources, reviewing evidence quality, deduplicating source intake, and generating cited preliminary briefs from auditable evidence cards.

View GitHub Portfolio

Overview

Research automation needs evidence controls first.

Problem

Research work often becomes scattered links, copied summaries, and unclear provenance. Automated synthesis is only useful when evidence can be reviewed.

Implemented

  • Research projects and source intake with provenance metadata
  • SQLAlchemy persistence and Alembic migrations
  • Per-project source deduplication by normalized URL or title
  • Source review statuses and verified-source-first brief generation
