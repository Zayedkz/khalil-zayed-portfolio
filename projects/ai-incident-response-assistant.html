AI Incident Response Assistant

Incident workflow service for typed event intake, ordered timelines, risk classification, impacted-service extraction, deterministic summaries, and recommended response actions.

View GitHub Portfolio

Overview

Operational evidence becomes a usable timeline.

Problem

During incidents, alerts, deploy notes, metrics, logs, and human updates arrive out of order. Teams need a shared timeline and clear next actions.

Implemented

  • FastAPI endpoints for incident creation and event intake
  • Typed sources for alerts, logs, metrics, deploys, and humans
  • Deterministic timeline ordering and risk classification
  • Recommended actions based on criticality and source signals
